JAKSTRADA SPAM


Regional Policies and Strategies of Water Supply System (Kebijakan dan Strategi Daerah Penyelenggaraan Sistem Penyediaan Air Minum/JAKSTRADA SPAM) is a document that serves as a reference for the Implementation of Regional SPAM by taking into account the social, economic and cultural conditions of the local community, as well as the environmental conditions of the surrounding area in an effort to realize community welfare by guaranteeing the basic needs of drinking water that meets quality requirements, quantity and affordability. Apart from that, it is also to support the Indonesian government's work to achieve its Sustainable Development Goal (SDG) targets to ensure access to water and sanitation for all (SDG 6) and to make cities and settlements inclusive, safe, resilient, and sustainable (SDG 11). The final results of JAKSTRADA SPAM are determined in local regulations (Perda Kabupaten Fakfak & Perwali Kota Salatiga).
