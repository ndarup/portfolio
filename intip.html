Government Agency Land in Indonesia


The Study on Improving the Quality Of Government Agency Land Database Towards Digital Transformation in Indonesia. Based on the urgency of preparing a quality database and inventory of government agency assets, a comprehensive study is needed so that it can produce a formula to answer problems/constraints and bridge the gap between current real conditions and the ideal conditions expected regarding the quality of government agency land databases. Thus, the objectives of this study are (1) to examine the condition and quality of government agency databases towards digital transformation, and (2) to design strategies to improve the quality of valid government agency land databases. This goal is in line with the INTIP program which is being implemented by the Ministry of ATR/BPN in order to realize the availability of an accurate and up-to-date government agency land database.
