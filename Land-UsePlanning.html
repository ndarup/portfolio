Land-Use Planning


Land use planning is used to explore potential and conceptualize potential development as well as monitor the ongoing process of sustainable development in efforts to adapt the climate change. Village land use planning in West Manggarai, NTT was developed on the basis of community participation using the Participatory Land Use Planning (PLUP) method. Through the PLUP approach, it is hoped that it can prioritize participatory and inclusive aspects, by giving respect to the local tenurial system (land ownership and control) and the use of existing land-use, so that spatial-based village planning can be carried out and provide benefits to the community in the context of sustainable village development. Apart from that, the existence of this land use plan will also be part of the strategy for determining intervention actions in efforts to protect and preserve important ecosystems in the study area..
