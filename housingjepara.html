Housing Identification in Disaster-Prone Areas


The study of Housing Identification in Disaster-Prone Areas was prepared to provide direction/recommendations and formulate policies in handling housing in disaster-prone locations or affected by program relocation in Jepara Regency. The objectives to be achieved from the study are: 1) Identifying the distribution of housing in disaster-prone areas; 2) Identifying the characteristics of housing in disaster-prone areas; and 3) Formulating policy recommendations for handling housing in disaster-prone areas in Jepara Regency.
